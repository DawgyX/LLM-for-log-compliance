Rule 1: No user should access the system outside of business hours (9 AM - 5 PM).
Rule 2: Admin privileges should not be granted to guest users.
Rule 3: Failed login attempts should not exceed 3 within a 10-minute window.
Rule 4: Confidential files should not be accessed by non-admin users.
Rule 5: Unauthorized IP addresses should not access the system.
Rule 6: Users should not access directories outside of their designated department.
